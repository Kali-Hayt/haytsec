### ğŸ§  ARP Behavior Recap

- **ARP Request** = Broadcast:  
  "Who has 192.168.0.147? Tell 192.168.0.193"

- **ARP Reply** = Unicast:  
  "192.168.0.147 is at 00:11:22:33:44:55"

- ARP only works within a **single broadcast domain** (same subnet)
